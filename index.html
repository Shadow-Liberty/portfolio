Web Designer huh?

Exactly, that means I’ve developed skills in the art of making appealing websites. I’ve always liked computers and for years I knew I wanted to work with them, but I never knew how exactly. After my first year of Graphic Design at the HEPL in Liege, Belgium it occurred to me that I loved coding more than Photoshopping images and what not. And here I am. Coding and designing for a living.

So, what do you do?

I am more of a front-end developer than a back-end programmer as I prefer design over programming. That’s not to say I don’t know any programming language. I’ve learned how to design from start to finish, beginning with sketching on paper to wire framing on softwares like Balsamiq then designing on Adobe Illustrator and Sketch. The rest happens in a code editor.
